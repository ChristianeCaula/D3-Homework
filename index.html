Explore the relationship between Income X Obesity and Income X Smoke

Using one year data from the website U.S. Census Bureau's American Community Survey we can explore the relationship between Income vs. Obesity and Income vs. Smoke for the US states.

We can see that the obesity tends to be higher in the states where the avarage income is lower and vice-versa.
